4Square: My Favourite Playground Game

This website is about a game: 4 Square, a super fun game and I love playing at parks with my friends. It uses a bouncy ball on a court a plit into four square, where players hit the ball into other square without letting it bounce twice. The goal is to stay in the higher square by getting others out, like if they miss or hit it out of bounds.

Want to learn the rules? Check out this guide: 4 Square Rules on Wikipedia
